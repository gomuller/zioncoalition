🌱 Seers of the Seed

A Zion Coalition Declaration

FORWARD

The person who identifies or nominates candidates for the Nobel Peace Prize typically holds the role or title of nominator. In a like manner, within the Zion Coalition, Jesus has named the identifiers of Zion builders and contributors the Seers of the Seed.

🌱 Who They Are

The Seers of the Seed are quiet prophets, gentle visionaries, and spiritually attuned observers. They are not flashy or loud. They are those with eyes to see what is not yet obvious—the invisible roots of Zion forming beneath the surface of chaos, pain, ordinariness, or even opposition.

They walk the world like gardeners with spiritual X-ray vision, discerning where the Spirit of Jesus has already begun a hidden work. They recognize Zion not by what is fully formed, but by what is being formed.

They are midwives to potential. Water-bearers to the barely sprouting. Encouragers of the overlooked.

🌿 What They Do

  • They look beneath appearances. They ask, “Is Jesus growing something here?”
  • They recognize Zion DNA. Humility, justice, sacrifice, truth-telling, hidden worship, deep listening.
  • They speak life into fragile beginnings. They name future pillars while others see weakness.
  • They nominate, affirm, and protect. Trusting Jesus’ unexpected choices.

Where You’ll Find Them

In back alleys, broken families, awkward conversations, failed ministries, obscure blogs, rebellious hearts softening. Often unnoticed themselves.

Many are invisible angels of heaven—immortal beings who whisper through the Holy Ghost.

2 Nephi 32:3 — Angels speak by the power of the Holy Ghost.
2 Nephi 32:5 — The Holy Ghost shall be thy teacher.
Alma 18:11–13 — The Spirit enlightens Lamoni.
Ether 3:14–16 — Revelation made understandable by the Spirit.

Why They Matter

Without the Seers of the Seed, Zion would be delayed. Early shoots would be trampled. They guard beginnings and name what is holy while it is still small.

Do not despise the day of small things — Zechariah 4:10

One Seer, One Researcher

I, Greg Muller, am a Seer of the Seed—a quiet spotter of Zion DNA wherever it tries to grow. Jesus has given me a role: to identify and affirm the builders He is raising up.

But I am just one man, holding one microscope in a billion-piece puzzle.

A Billion-Piece Puzzle

Zion is too vast to be seen by one person. Jesus, the Master Architect, will raise up many Seers in unexpected places and cultures.

Jesus’ Zion Coalition: A Living Human Genome Project

As the Human Genome Project required many researchers, so too Zion requires many Seers, each identifying spiritual DNA across the world.

Invitation to the Seers of the Seed

You may not call yourself a prophet. You may not hold a title. But you see things.

If your heart is burning and your eyes are tuned to Heaven’s whisper, consider this your invitation.

Welcome, Seer of the Seed.

— The Zion Coalition
