Jesus TV Altar

Jesus TV — is a parable and a choice

It’s no accident that almost all of us now carry a tiny “TV” in our hands.

📱

Just as Jesus once gave humanity the alphabet so we could choose what to feed our minds, He’s now placed in our palms a small electric remote control—our cell-phone TV—so we can choose with even greater intention what we let into our souls.

In this sense, TV has become a modern spiritual altar, a tool Jesus has allowed so that each of us can decide what spirit we invite.

The device is neutral. The choice is not. Our use of it becomes our worship, and ultimately, our destiny.

JesusVerse: The Documentary — Episode 1

🎵 JESUS THE VERY THOUGHT OF THEE 🎵

“We all live every day in virtual environments, defined by our ideas… by what we are thinking of… by our thoughts… and now we enter into a new gospel dispensation of powerful virtual-reality-generating tools. Exciting. Scary. Inevitable.”

This new, virtual scripture highlights how our perceptions of reality—our Jesus WORLD—are created and constantly being re-created by what we watch, the narratives, beliefs, concepts, sacred imaginations, and holy guesses we hold in our minds and hearts.

What Jesus and I have spoken is not the normal, conventional language used to describe the gospel. However, it is a new gospel language Jesus has certain informal people speak—for a wise purpose in Him.

Narrator (Greg):

Welcome to the JesusVerse. Welcome to the story of a world within a galaxy of worlds—a place where Jesus is already weaving His presence into every life.

This is the beginning of My Jesus-Greg World—but really, it’s bigger than me. It’s about all of us. Because whether we realize it or not, everybody is building a Jesus world with Jesus.

The main way Jesus builds your respective Jesus WORLD is by broadcasting to your mind and heart recurring episodes of Jesus TV—episodes so highly immersive, interactive, and carefully orchestrated that they almost feel like ultimate reality.

But the good news of the gospel is that each episode can be hacked, changed, transformed—miraculously.

The Hidden Architect

Some people know it. Some don’t. Some deny it. But the truth is: every story, every act of creation, every flicker of love or sacrifice bears His fingerprints.

Jesus is not passive. He is orchestrating. He is the hidden Author, the quiet Designer, the invisible Architect.

Every life is a canvas He has touched, even when we don’t give Him credit.

He works through our choices, our failures, our improv, even our unbelief. He respects our freedom, yet still leaves traces of Himself everywhere.

“All things were made by Him.”

When John wrote these words, he wasn’t just talking about mountains and stars. He was talking about your life—your friendships, your work, your imagination, your digital worlds, and your physical spaces.

You are building a Jesus world because He gave you breath, imagination, and the power to shape.

Everything Can Become an Altar

The world you’re building isn’t silent—it already sings.

Every person, every place, every thing can become an altar—set apart, tuned, waiting to reveal its voice.

  • A door
  • A pillow
  • A piece of bread
  • A protest march
  • A sunrise

Jesus has written songs into all of them.

All you need is the gift of prophecy to hear them. The gift of sacred imagination to invoke them.

Good, better, and best songs—you get to choose which you’ll sing: a MORE-Jesus song, or a LESS-Jesus song.

📡 Opening Broadcast:

From the moment I was born I started watching Jesus TV.

The first channel I watched was the Jesus-Pauline Reality Show.

I watched continually as my mom, Pauline Muller, breastfed me, cooed at me, and whispered love into my soul. Jesus was broadcasting through her—through her touch, tone, and tears.

Though I didn’t have language yet, my spirit was already tuned in.

Jesus TV was alive in her eyes, her heartbeat, her sacrifice.

I didn’t know it yet, but I was being formed not just by milk—but by presence.

And that presence? It was Jesus, inhabiting the ordinary.
