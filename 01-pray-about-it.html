“Did you think to pray?”

Some Wild Claims Are Normal for God

Some things at Zion Coalition may sound unusual, mystical, symbolic, or even impossible at first glance.

That’s okay.

Scripture is full of burning bushes, prophetic dreams, angels, impossible healings, resurrections, visions, heavenly encounters, strange symbols, and mysteries hidden in plain sight.

“Call unto me, and I will answer thee, and show thee great and mighty things, which thou knowest not.” — Jeremiah 33:3

God has always spoken in ways that stretch human understanding.

Don’t Just Believe Everything

We are not asking you to blindly accept every claim you see here.

Test it. Pray about it. Seek God directly.

Christianity is not meant to be passive consumption. It is meant to be relationship.

If something sounds strange:

  • Pray sincerely.
  • Ask God for wisdom.
  • Search the Scriptures.
  • Look for truth with humility.
  • Pay attention to what bears good fruit.

How to Test Things Spiritually

  1. Pray honestly.
    God is not offended by honest questions.
  2. Look for alignment with Christ.
    Does it move toward truth, repentance, love, humility, mercy, justice, and holiness?
  3. Watch the fruit.
    Good trees bear good fruit.
  4. Do not fear mystery.
    Not everything meaningful can be reduced immediately into simple explanations.
  5. Stay grounded.
    We value Scripture, wisdom, discernment, and spiritual maturity.

The Invitation

Zion Coalition is ultimately an invitation:

  • Seek God sincerely.
  • Learn to discern.
  • Grow spiritually.
  • Remain humble.
  • Stay awake.
“Ask, and it shall be given you; seek, and ye shall find; knock, and it shall be opened unto you.” — Matthew 7:7

Don’t just react emotionally to strange things.

Pray about them.
